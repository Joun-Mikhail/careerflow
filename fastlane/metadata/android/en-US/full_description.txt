CareerFlow turns your job search into something you can actually reason about.

Track every application from wishlist to offer on a Kanban pipeline board. Log
interviews with date, type, interviewer, and outcome. Capture follow-up tasks
with priorities and due dates. Save notes and resume versions per application.
Compare offers side by side. Watch your conversion rates trend over time.

Highlights:
• Eight-stage pipeline (Wishlist → Applied → Assessment → Interview → Final → Offer → Rejected → Accepted)
• Interviews and tasks with reminders
• Offer tracking with base salary, bonus, equity, and decision status
• Per-application notes and secure attachments
• Dashboard and analytics: applications over time, status mix, conversion rates
• Light and dark themes
• Open source — github.com/Joun-Mikhail/careerflow
