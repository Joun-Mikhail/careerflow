CareerFlow turns a noisy job search into a clear pipeline.

Manage every application from wishlist to offer on a Kanban board. Log each
interview round, track follow-up tasks with priorities and due dates, save
notes and resume versions per application, compare offers side by side, and
watch your conversion rates trend over time.

Highlights
• Eight-stage pipeline board (Wishlist → Applied → Assessment → Interview → Final → Offer → Rejected → Accepted)
• Interviews and tasks with reminders
• Offer tracking with base salary, bonus, equity, benefits, and decision
• Per-application notes and secure attachments
• Dashboard and analytics: applications over time, status mix, conversion rates
• Light and dark themes
• Same data on web and mobile — your account syncs everywhere

Privacy-respecting and open source. Source at github.com/Joun-Mikhail/careerflow.
