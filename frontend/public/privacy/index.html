CareerFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

CareerFlow ("the Service") is an open-source job-search tracker. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, where it is stored, and how to delete it.

Who we are

CareerFlow is maintained by Joun Mikhail and the open-source contributors on GitHub. For privacy questions or data-deletion requests, contact gptmath55@gmail.com.

Data we store

When you create an account, the Service stores:

DataSourcePurpose
Email addressSignupAccount login
Password hash (bcrypt)Your passwordAuthentication
Full name (optional)SettingsPersonalising the UI
Job-search recordsYou enter themApplications, interviews, tasks, notes, offers
File attachmentsYou upload themResume and document storage
Session tokensServerMaintaining login
Audit log entriesServerSecurity monitoring

We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, contacts, microphone or camera input, location data, or third-party analytics beyond Sentry (optional, error reporting only when enabled by the operator).

Where data lives

  • Database — PostgreSQL on the operator's infrastructure.
  • Attachments — server disk under the configured upload directory.
  • Tokens — device localStorage (web) or platform key/value store (mobile).

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or brokers.

How long we keep data

Account data is retained while the account exists. Audit logs are kept for 90 days in the reference deployment. Delete your account from Settings or email gptmath55@gmail.com.

Children

CareerFlow is intended for users aged 16 and over.

Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. API traffic uses HTTPS. Auth endpoints are rate-limited. Report vulnerabilities via our Security Policy.

Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

You may access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Contact gptmath55@gmail.com.
